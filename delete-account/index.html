Gravity Sudoku may create an anonymous multiplayer account to store ELO, match history, and avatar selection for online features. If you want this server-side data deleted, use the process below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email gravitysudoku.help@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Delete Gravity Sudoku account.
  3. Include any details that help identify your account, such as your in-game nickname, approximate ELO, and platform.

What gets deleted

  • Anonymous multiplayer account identifier
  • Stored ELO / ranking data
  • Match history tied to that account
  • Server-side avatar/profile data associated with multiplayer

What may remain

  • Local data stored only on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data
  • Records we may need to retain temporarily for fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling

Response time

We aim to review deletion requests within a reasonable time. If we need more information to verify the request, we may reply by email.
