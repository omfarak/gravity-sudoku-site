This Privacy Policy explains how Gravity Sudoku collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

1. What we collect

Gravity Sudoku stores gameplay progress, settings, and local statistics on your device. For multiplayer, analytics, crash reporting, ads, and purchase restoration, third-party services may process device-level and service-related data.

2. Local app data

Saved games, progression, settings, and related gameplay state are primarily stored locally on your device. Removing the app may delete this information unless your platform backup restores it.

3. Third-party services

Gravity Sudoku currently uses these external service providers:

  • Google AdMob for ads and consent flows
  • Google Firebase Analytics for product analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash diagnostics
  • Firebase Performance for app performance telemetry
  • Supabase for multiplayer/auth-related backend features
  • Apple App Store / Google Play for purchase processing

4. Ads and tracking

The free version of the app may show ads. Ad partners may process identifiers, IP-based approximate location, consent state, and device metadata to serve or measure ads, subject to their own policies and platform permissions.

5. Purchases

If you buy "Remove Ads", payment is handled by Apple or Google. We do not receive your full payment credentials.

6. Children

Gravity Sudoku is not intentionally designed to collect personal information from children. Parents or guardians who have concerns can contact us using the support page.

7. Your choices

You can limit ad personalization through your device settings, restore purchases through the app, request multiplayer account deletion through our delete-account page, and stop using the service at any time by uninstalling the app.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date on this page.

9. Contact

Support and privacy questions: gravitysudoku.help@gmail.com or Gravity Sudoku Support. For multiplayer account/data deletion requests, visit Delete Account.
